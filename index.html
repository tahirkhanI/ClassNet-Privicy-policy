Privacy Policy - ClassNest

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to ClassNest, your all-in-one classroom resource management app. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy outlines:

  • What data we collect and how we use it.
  • How we store and secure your data.
  • Your rights regarding your data.
  • How to contact us for concerns or inquiries.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register or use our services, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Phone number (optional, for OTP login/recovery if implemented via Firebase)
  • User role (e.g., Student, Faculty, Administrator) for access control
  • Associated institution/department (optional, for organizational purposes)

1.2 Usage Data

  • IP address and device info
  • App usage statistics (e.g., resources viewed, bookings made, features used)
  • Activity timestamps
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

1.3 Resource Data

  • For Administrators: Details of resources added (name, type, quantity, location, availability status).
  • For Users: Records of resources booked, checked out, and returned.

1.4 Location Data (if implemented)

If features requiring location are enabled (e.g., finding resources in specific campus buildings), we may collect:

  • General location data (e.g., campus building, room number) if explicitly provided or enabled by the user for resource tracking or pick-up/drop-off.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate users securely via Firebase (if applicable).
  • Enable seamless browsing, booking, and management of classroom resources.
  • Track resource availability and usage.
  • Manage booking schedules and notifications.
  • Send updates, reminders (e.g., for returns), and important announcements.
  • Provide administrative tools for resource management and oversight.
  • Enhance app performance, user experience, and reliability.
  • Improve resource allocation and identify popular/underutilized resources.

3. Data Security

Security measures include:

  • Firebase Authentication (if used) or other secure authentication methods
  • HTTPS communication for data transfer
  • Role-based access control for different user types (Student, Faculty, Administrator)
  • Regular updates and security audits to protect against vulnerabilities
  • Data encryption where appropriate

4. Your Rights

You can:

  • View and update your profile information.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Delete your account (this will remove your personal data from our active databases, though some historical usage data might be anonymized and retained for analytics).
  • Opt out of non-essential communications (e.g., promotional messages, if any).

6. Children's Privacy

ClassNest is intended for university students, faculty, and staff. It is not designed for children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. All changes will be reflected in-app and on our website, with the "Last updated" date revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
